# Hi, I'm Liz.

I'm building VibeFlow Marketing — an AI marketing OS for indie hackers who'd rather be shipping product than wrestling with launch pages, ad copy, ASO, SEO, and the rest.

This blog is where I'll write about that build, and the marketing problem I'm trying to solve.

## What you'll find here

A few different shapes of post, all in service of the same goal — making indie marketing actually doable.

- **Build-in-public notes.** What I shipped this week, what broke, what I learned. Including the unflattering bits.
- **Marketing playbooks for builders.** The actual tactics that move a needle for a one-person team — written in code-brain language, not consultant-speak.
- **Dogfooding posts.** I run my own SEO agent, ASO agent, and content agent on VibeFlow itself. When I do, the output gets posted here so you can see what the tool produces against a real target.

## What you won't find

- Listicles I wouldn't read myself
- "10 hacks for…" anything
- AI-generated filler content with no point of view (*— ironic, given what VibeFlow does, but the output here gets a human edit pass before it ships*)

## A note on the meta

This first post is hand-written. From the next one on, everything goes through VibeFlow first, gets edited by me, and ships. That's the test — if my own product can't help me write a blog, the product isn't ready.

If you're an indie hacker, a vibe coder, or just someone who's tired of watching a beautifully built product die because the marketing never landed — you're who I'm building this for. Stick around.

— Liz
